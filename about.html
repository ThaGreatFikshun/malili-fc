About Our Team

Learn about the history, mission, and values of our MFC.

Our History

2019

Founded with a vision to bring the community together through football.

2020

Increased local participation and held our first community tournament.

2021

Expanded our youth programs and started our women's team.

2022

Achieved regional recognition and won several local championships.

2023

Started our youth academy and became a staple in the local sports community.

Mission & Vision

Mission

To foster a love for football in our community and build a team that excels both on and off the field.

Vision

To be a leading football club known for its strong community ties, outstanding performance, and commitment to development.

Core Values

Teamwork

We believe in the power of working together to achieve common goals.

Passion

Our love for football drives us to excel and inspire others.

Excellence

We strive for the highest standards in all that we do.

Integrity

We uphold the highest ethical standards in our actions and decisions.

Respect

We value and respect everyone’s contributions and differences.

Innovation

We are committed to continuously improving and adapting.
